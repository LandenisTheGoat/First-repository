How To Play Bottleflip.io

  1. Use the right arrow key to flip the bottle
  2. hold the right arrow key to do more that one flip
  3. If you land a singal flip you will get 100 points
  4. If you land a double flip you will get 300 points
  5. If you land a triple flip you will get 500 points
  6. If you land a quadruple flip you will get 1000 points
